# Citizen App Help

The citizen experience in this repo is represented by the simulation app and guided recovery surfaces.

## Citizen Journey

- Give consent before protected simulation access.
- Receive drill, alert, or honeypot safety handoff.
- Review recovery guidance, dispute letters, and referrals when needed.

## Support Notes

- Help content should be shown in simple language.
- Consent and privacy state must be visible before sensitive workflows begin.
- Escalate to bank, legal, or mental-health referral flows when harm has already occurred.
