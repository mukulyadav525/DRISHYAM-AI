# Legal Evidence Workflow

Evidence workflows package fraud intelligence into formats usable by police, partners, and recovery teams.

## Included Artifacts

- FIR-ready graph bundles
- 65B-style export paths from actions
- dispute letters and recovery packets
- deepfake and UPI evidence summaries

## Escalation

- verify chain of events
- export only the required evidence set
- route the case to legal or police support handbooks when escalation is needed
