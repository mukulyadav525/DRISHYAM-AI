# Annotation Guidelines

Annotation standards are needed for fraud taxonomy, scam entities, deepfake signals, and drill outcome labels.

## Required Labeling Rules

- label scam type, urgency tactic, authority claim, payment ask, and extraction target
- record confidence and ambiguity when intent is unclear
- separate observed facts from analyst interpretation

## Quality Control

- use reviewer sign-off for sensitive fraud or victim-harm categories
- track drift and update taxonomy in the continuous-improvement cycle
