# Telecom FAQ

## What is the telecom-facing value?
Earlier scam detection, IVR safety interventions, and coordinated alert delivery.

## Are telecom flows live?
The repo uses sandbox-safe integrations and rollout planning rather than live operator credentials.

## What should telecom teams review first?
`docs/telecom/telecom_integration.md`, the onboarding playbook, and escalation ownership.
