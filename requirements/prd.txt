SECTION 1 — PRODUCT OVERVIEW
================================================================================

One-Line Positioning: India's first end-to-end AI anti-scam operating system —
                     from detection to recovery, for every citizen on every device.

Target Users
------------
  - Citizens (smartphone + feature phone + no-device)
  - Telecom operators (Jio, Airtel, Vi, BSNL)
  - Banks and NBFCs
  - Police and cyber cells (state + national)
  - Government agencies (MHA, DoT, RBI, NPCI, CERT-In, SEBI)
  - Insurers
  - Job platforms
  - NGOs and legal aid bodies

Non-Negotiables
---------------
  - Privacy-first architecture (DPDP Act compliant)
  - Feature phone and 2G support
  - Support for all 22 scheduled Indian languages
  - No illegal surveillance
  - No forced interception
  - No non-consensual data resale

Versioning
----------
  - Module versioning: M<N>-v<X>.<Y>
  - Release versioning: SENTINEL-<YEAR>.<SPRINT>.<PATCH>
  - Document versioning: PRD-v<X>.<Y>
